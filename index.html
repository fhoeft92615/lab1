Introduction

Bread has been around for thousands of years, and there are so many different kinds to make. The most basic recipe requires just four ingredients; flour, yeast, salt, and water. This recipe is so simple, it doesn't even need to be kneaded. All you need is a dutch oven and time; 18 hours to be exact!
